Canine Servicess Website

Online Pet's Services

Project Overview


Concept: The Canine Services website is a fully responsive platform designed to cater to the needs of dog owners and service providers. The website allows clients to browse available services, book appointments, and manage their canine care needs online.

Key Features

  • Dog Breeds Display: Showcase various dog breeds and their specific services.
  • Service Booking: Clients can book appointments online for services such as walking, bathing, training, food, grooming/hostel, and veterinarian visits.
  • Auto-Generated Emails: Automatic email notifications are sent upon booking confirmation.
  • Social Media Integration: Integrated Instagram and YouTube APIs to display reels directly on the website.
  • WhatsApp Integration: A contact feature that redirects users to WhatsApp for direct communication.
  • Admin Panel: Separate admin panel for managing images, confirming appointments, and handling other administrative tasks.

Technologies Used

-The website was developed using a combination of PHP, HTML, JavaScript, MySQL, Bootstrap, and AJAX. These technologies were chosen to ensure a dynamic, responsive, and user-friendly experience.
